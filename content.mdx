## Project Overview

**Signlys** is an open-source PDF signing tool built with Next.js and Tailwind CSS. The goal is to provide a seamless experience for users who want to sign documents digitally without the hassle of complex software or expensive subscriptions.

### Key Features

- **Intuitive Layout:** The interface is designed to feel familiar, similar to popular editing applications, so users can get started quickly.
- **Signature Copier:** Easily copy and paste your signature across all pages of your document, saving time and effort.
- **Open Source:** The entire project is open source and available on GitHub. Contributions are welcome!

## How It Works

- **Start Signing:** Simply upload your document and begin placing signatures with just a few clicks.
- **Responsive Design:** The app works well on both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring accessibility wherever you are.
- **Night Mode:** Toggle between light and dark themes for comfortable viewing at any time.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js** for server-side rendering and routing
- **Tailwind CSS** for rapid UI development
- **NextUI** for beautiful, accessible components
- **Lucide Icons** for modern, customizable icons
